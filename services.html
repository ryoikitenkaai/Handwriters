How It Works

Your Publication Journey

A transparent, step-by-step process from first consultation to final acceptance.

01

Initial Consultation

Free consultation to understand your research goals, field, existing manuscript and target indexing. We design a custom publication roadmap for you.

02

Manuscript Preparation

Our domain-expert writers craft or refine your manuscript — proper structure, polished language, complete references, journal-specific formatting, plagiarism-free.

03

Journal Shortlisting & Approval

We curate best-fit journals based on scope, impact factor, acceptance rates and open-access options. You choose — we only proceed with your sign-off.

04

Submission & Communication

We handle the full submission — account creation, manuscript upload, cover letter and all post-submission correspondence with journal editors on your behalf.

05

Revision & Resubmission

Our expert team addresses reviewer comments point-by-point, strengthens the manuscript, and prepares a compelling response letter before resubmission.

06

Accepted & Published 🎉

We guide you through APC payment, proof checks and final publication. You receive your DOI and the paper goes live in the indexed database.
