Our Publication Process

We ensure a seamless, transparent, and efficient process for getting your research published.

1

Provide Indexing Requirements

Share your indexing requirements to ensure the paper aligns with your academic goals.

2

Share the Scope of the Paper

Provide details about the subject area, focus, and objectives to help us understand its domain.

3

Discuss the Timeline

Define expectations for acceptance, revision, and publication phases.

4

Shortlist Journals

We curate a list of journals matching your paper’s field, ensuring relevance and high acceptance chances.

5

Verify and Finalize the Journal

Review and finalize the journal based on authenticity, indexing, and other factors.

6

Receive Acceptance Notification

We update you on the journal’s decision regarding your paper submission.

7

Pay the Article Processing Charges (APC)

Once accepted, pay the APC to proceed with the publication.

8

Submit the Final Revision

Submit the revised version based on feedback from reviewers and editors.

9

Receive the Paper URL

Get the official URL of your published paper for sharing and record-keeping.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your publication guarantee?

We guarantee 100% publication once your paper is accepted by the journal. With our expert services, we proudly maintain a 95% acceptance rate.

When should I pay the APC (Article Processing Charge)?

The APC is payable only after your paper gets published. We do not charge any submission fees.

How do you assist with the publication process?

We are a third-party service provider that helps make your paper ready for publication. After you submit your paper, we ensure it meets all journal requirements and is refined for acceptance.

Do you have any service charges?

Yes, our service charges are variable and depend on the work required for your paper. The more detailed the work needed, the higher the charge.
